Important Information

The J.B. Hunt Hackathon is a competition that is hosted in conjunction with the Association of Computing Machinery (ACM) at the University of Arkansas twice each year. The competition consists of teams competing against each other to develop a working prototype that is presented in front of a panel of J.B. Hunt judges. Participants are not required to have a computer science background - all majors are welcome!
